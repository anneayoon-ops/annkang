Client Universal Studios, Illumination

Minions

Project
Motion Graphic Sing Along Sequences & Experience Design

Job Role
Lead Design, Game Design & Illustration

Skills Used
Photoshop & Illustrator
To further expand the experience of watching ’Minions' for the audience, I made a sing-along and game. The sing-along interchanges between 2D and 3D designs for variety. The Minions game is inspired by the old 8 bit games and has simple yet colorful urban buildings.
