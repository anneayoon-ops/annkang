presidentgraves.com

To promote the TV show 'Graves', I created the Presidential Library. This website provides immersive information from the show to describe how to visit the library, the history behind it, and the family biography of President Graves. For the user to be further intrigued, there’s a separate part of the website that breaks the fourth wall describing the profile of the actors, yet it still keeps to the overall aesthetic.

  • Involved from concept to launch the website
  • Worked on with Art Director Gregg, Producers, and Developers
