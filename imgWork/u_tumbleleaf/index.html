Amazon Studios Tumble Leaf App

This is a Kindle application which integrates with the educational curriculum from the Amazon kids show called Tumble Leaf. The application is designed for kids to explore, learn, and have fun while playing the games. The twelve simple touch screen activities were composed to help preschoolers learn about reflection, force, motion, and so on.

  • Involved from brainstorming for pitch to launch, from concept to visual mockup
  • Designed storyboards for game task flows
  • Worked on with Art Director Gregg, Designer Bao and Producers
