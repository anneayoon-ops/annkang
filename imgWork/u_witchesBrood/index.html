thewitchesbrood.com

The Brood website is to promote the movie ’the Last Witch Hunter’ from Lionsgate. The user can witness behind-the-scenes secret stories of witches and the culture of the New York coven from the movie, but in the format of a blog run by a side character from the movie. I designed the interactive experiences to display the overall dark atmosphere of witches. The user can explore interviews, articles, and games throughout the uniquely designed website.

  • Involved from brainstorming for pitch to launch, from concept to visual mockup
  • Designed and created typography and visual assets
  • Worked on with Art Director Gregg, Producers and Developers
